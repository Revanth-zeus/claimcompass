Vision
Adjusters Spend 60% of Their Time Contextualizing Files
Every new liability claim starts the same way — an adjuster opens the ACORD intake, cross-references the carrier, checks what evidence has arrived, figures out what's missing, and decides what to do next. ClaimCompass compresses that entire first pass into seconds. Upload an ACORD Form 3, get a structured claim record, a liability-keyed evidence checklist, a transparent state classification, and real regulatory validation — all with an audit trail for every transition.
Technology
Built Different
01

Deterministic Parsing

Drop an ACORD Form 3 PDF. The system extracts 26 structured fields via pypdf — liability type, premises/products branch, insured and claimant info, occurrence details, injury severity signals — with zero LLM calls on fillable forms. Every parse logs which extraction path it took.

02

Rules-Based Classification

Six claim lifecycle stages — Intake, Investigation, Reserve Setting, Negotiation, Litigation Track, Resolution — classified using explicit boolean rules. When it says "Investigation," you can read the exact conditions that fired. No black boxes. A 20-year veteran can agree or push back on specific rules.

03

Evidence Chain

Every document upload, every checklist change, every state transition generates a provenance entry. What triggered it, what changed, which rule fired. Not "the system decided" — the system shows its work, with a full audit trail from intake to resolution.

04

Live Regulatory Validation

Carrier NAIC codes validated against the NAIC Listing. TX DOI complaint indexes pulled from the Texas Open Data Portal. CPSC recall history queried live from SaferProducts.gov for products liability claims. Real external data, real sources, no fabricated statistics.
